Personal Development Guide

Health

Drink at least 2-3 liters of water daily.

Exercise for 30 minutes to maintain physical fitness.

Sleep for 7-8 hours every night.

Learning

Practice coding for at least one hour daily.

Read technical books to improve understanding.

Review concepts regularly to strengthen memory.

Productivity

Create a daily task list every morning.

Focus on one task at a time to avoid distractions.

Track progress and reflect at the end of the day.

Goals

Set short-term and long-term goals clearly.

Break big goals into smaller achievable tasks.

Evaluate progress weekly and adjust plans if needed.
